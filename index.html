Our Mission

Everchange seeks to make it easier for everyone to contribute to a cause they care about. By simply registering your debit/credit card Everchange rounds up each purchase you make and donates that money to the charities of your choice.

Register Your Cards

Simply signup for an Everchange account and register the cards you use to make everyday purchases with a one time $1 setup fee

Choose Your Charities

With our platform you are not limited to a single charity and can use Donation Diversifaction Portfolios to select what charities and what percentage of your round ups go to each charity!

Watch Your Donations Grow

See how much money you have donated to charities and at the end of the year receive a tax receipt for your charitable contributions!

Donation Diversifaction Portfolios

Donation Diversifaction Portfolios aim to make it easier to donate to multiple charities you care about. We offer preset Portfolios along with the ability to create your own portoflio and decide where you money goes to help make a difference to the causes that most directly impact you.
